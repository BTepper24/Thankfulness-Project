What Am I Thankful For?

I am thankful for my friends and family who help me overcome obstacles throughout life. I am thankful that I was able to get together with my family for Thanksgiving, and I am thankful for the food we had!

It is important to be thankful and able to recognize your blessings because it makes for a healthier mental life. If one is constantly wishing for things, they won't have time to appreciate what they have. I believe this is the true meaning of Thanksgiving!
